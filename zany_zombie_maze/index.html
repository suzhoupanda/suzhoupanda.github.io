Zany Zombie Maze

App Information

Intro
A deadly virus has broken out and infected all of humanity. Rescue a team of scientists and help them acquire all of the tools and materials they need to research a cure for the zombie virus. In the mean time, avoid crossing the path of the dozens of zombies roaming the landscape or else they will follow you until they can shoot you to oblivion. Kill mutant zombies before the virus can mutate and evade the research efforts of scientists. Complete a series of missions in this top-down shooter mini-game and save humanity from a dystopian nightmare where zombies dictate the lives of humans.

Game FAQs and Common Troubleshooting Issues

How do I move the player? How do I shoot enemies?

Touch anywhere on the screen to adjust the player's compass orientation as well as to make the player move in the desired direction. Press on the gun icon in the lower left-hand corner to fire bullets.

Player Movement/ControlHelp Topics
How do I activate collectibles?

Click on the menu icon and select "View Inventory". Tap on the collectible which you wish to activate. A detail window will come into view, which should display an activation switch that allows for the collectible item in question to be activated.

Weapons and Collectibles Help Topics
How do I save a game? How do I load saved games?

Saved games can be loaded from the main menu. After you select a player profile, the saved game sessions associated with that profile can be accessed via the "Load Saved Game" button. To save a game, click on the menu button during gameplay, and click the "Save Game" button. Games will automatically be saved when the app moves into the background, wehther by accident or purposeful decision. In addition, games are automatically saved when a memory overload forces the app to quit the current game session.

Saved Games Help Topics
How can I see statistics for previously played game sessions?

Click the "Select Player Profile" button in the main menu, which should display all the player profiles that have been created by the user. Tap the arrow icon associated with the tableview cell associated with a player profile in order to display a list of player statics for previously completed game sessions.

Player Statistics Help Topics
How do I create a player profile?

A player profile can be created by tapping the "Create Player Profile" button or by first clicking "Select Player Profile" button and then tapping the plus bar button in the upper right corner of the screen. Tapping the "Edit" button allows users to edit a pre-existing player profile without damaging previously saved game data and player statistics.

Player Profile Help Topics
How do I increase life level? How do I earn money? How do I get more bullets?

Life level is increased by collecting rice bowls. Money is earned by collecting red envelopes or other collectibles items with intrinsic monetary value. Bullets are acquired by collecting bullet icons.
