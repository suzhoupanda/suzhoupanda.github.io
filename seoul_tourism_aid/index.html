Seoul Tourism Aid

App Information

Korean Language Help
Listen to native-speaker audio samples for common phrases related to transportation, food, restaurants, and other topics. Record spoken speech and translate to Korean, or simply type a message in your native language and translate to Korean. (Translation functionality is powered by Google Translate).
Flickr Image Galleries
View images organized by categories related to different areas of Korean culture: K-pop bands, Korean food, etc. Each search item is related to keyword phrases customized to download images specifically relating to the specific areas of Korean culture and filter out any outliers.
Brainy Bunny Learns Korean
Play "Brain Bunny Learns Korean," a simple 2D side-scrolling platformer where you must collect different kinds of objects and identify their correct Korean name. One point earned for each object whose Korean word is correctly identified.
Product Prices
View prices for common products in Korea and search via Apple Maps for nearby locations where the items may be sold. Each item category will randomly display product price and other information for a variety of products when clicked. Prices are converted to the native speaker's currency based on the current exchange ratio.
Region Monitoring
For major tourist regions, make use of Apple's location services and receive notifications when entering and leaving the proximity of a tourist site. Region monitoring for a specific site can be toggled via the "Region Monitoring" option in the main menu, or via the detail page relating to a specific tourist site, which is accessed via the "Seoul Tourism" option in the main menu.
Navigation Aids
Look up places belonging to various categories (e.g. coffee shop, pub/bar, convenience store, etc.) within specific areas of Seoul (e.g. Itaewon, Insadong, Mapo-gu, etc). Information for places can viewed in tableview format or as annotation visible on the maps for the region selected. In addition, access navigation aids for specific tourist areas (e.g. Seoul Grand Park, Korean War Memorial, etc.), which allows users to view specific points of interest within the region and view pictures and detail information related to that place.
Weather Forecasts
Get up-to-date weather forecasts for major cities in Korea. Specify the forecast date and period. Forecast information thumbnails provide short summaries and can be clicked to access more detailed weather information for the speicific day being forecasted.
YouTube Tourism Videos
Search and view videos related to Korean tourism by interacting with YouTube. A built-in YouTube player allows videos of interest to be played based on user-defined or pre-defined search criteria.

FAQs and Common Troubleshooting Issues

What is region monitoring? How do I use region monitoring to find out if I'm close to a tourist site or not??

Region

Region Monitoring allows you to receive notifications when you enter or leave important tourist sites. Region monitoring can be turned on or off in one of two ways: (1) select the "Region Monitoring" option from the App Main Menu and then toggle the switch corresponding to a tourist site in the table view, (2) toggle the switch displayed on the detail page for a tourist site that is displayed after selecting the site from tourist site collection view, which is accessed via the "Seoul Tourism" option from the App Main Menu. When region monitoring is toggled on, you will be prompted to select a monitoring distances of 50, 100, or 200 meters. Shorter distances mean that you will be notified when you are closer to the site.Help Topics
When do I receive notifications when I am close to a tourist site? Why didn't I receive a notification?

Certain conditions have to be met for the user to receive notifications for region boundary crossings. The user has to cross the boundary, move away from the boundary a minimum distance, and also remain away from the boundary crossing for a certain period of time. This is to prevent the user getting notifications haphazardly when the user is close to the boundary region and may be moving in and out of the region unwittingly. In addition to the user-specified region monitoring distances, there is an additional cushion distance of about 200 meters that must be crossed for notifications to occur.

Region Monitroing Help Topics
Why doesn't region monitoring work for my app?

Region monitoring may not work for one of several reasons: (1) The device may not have the hardware needed to support region monitoring, (2) the user denied the app authorization for region monitoring,(3) the user disabled location services in the Settings app, (4) the user disabled background refresh in the Settings app, either for the Seoul Tourism Aid specifically or for the entire device, (5) The device may be in airplane mode. When in airplane mode, the app cannot power up the necessary hardware to support region monitoring. Therefore, check the Setting app to make sure that location services and background refresh is available for the app. Also, check that the app is not in airplane mode.

When is a player considered to have lost the game?

When health level falls below zero, the player is considered to have lost the game. Once the player's health falls below zero, the player plane will not be able to achieve a game win by contacting the endpoint letter. Conversely, if the player contacts the endpont letter, then subsequent damage cannot reduce player health to the point of causing the player to lose the game.

Game Rules Help Topics
How can the player be moved?

The player moves vertically by tilting the phone away from or towards the user. The player's horizontal speed can be adjusted by tapping to the right or left of the player. The player can move in the forward direction or the reverse direction, but propeller speed only changes for movement in the forward direction.

Player Movement Help Topics
How can the player be killed?

The player is killed when health level falls below zero. When the player plane's health falls below zero, it will cease to fly forward and fall to the ground and after a period of a few seconds will explode, at which point the user can restart the level or return to the main level selection menu.

How can the player's health status be determined?

The hearts in the upper left of the screen indicate player health. A full heart indicates 2 health points. A player whose health has decreased to 4 will begin emitting smoke, and a player who has just been hurt recently will fade in and out repeatedly.

How can the player be hurt?

They player is hurt each time contact is made with a harmful barrier (e.g. spike or poisonous lollipop), or if the player collides with an enemy. After being hurt, the player is invulnerable for a short period of time, during which he is unaffected by any additional collisions with the enemies.
